Audio

Montbummery runs Avid Pro Tools 12 and is gradually building a small collection of recording gear. Currently they're capable of producing podcasts, audiobooks, soundtracks, radio advertising and small solo music projects (folk, hip-hop, electronic) all the way through from recording to mixing and mastering. As they upgrade their equipment they hope to tackle bigger projects including working with sound in Unity and MaxMSP as well as recording full bands. In addition to their personal work on Soundcloud, Montbummery has logged over 120 hours of studio time recording classical performances for the WVU School of Music and at least triple that speaking and spinning tunes on-air for WWVU-FM.
