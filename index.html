About

Jackson Montgomery is a recent graduate of the West Virginia University Eberly College of Arts and Sciences. They earned their Bachelor of Multidisciplinary Studies with concentrations in public relations, music technology and philosophy.

Their work includes experiments in many forms of modern and traditional media including digital video, printmaking, web, digital audio, tape recording, liquid light projection, paint and sculpture. They are interested in exploring the possibilities of sound in virtual or augmented reality environments and leading creative teams in this pursuit.

Montbummery is available for a wide range of creative projects, editing, voice acting, and more. Please direct any work requests to montbummery@gmail.com for a timely response.
